Our Story & Mission

The full story of Code-X Circle

Our Origin Story

Code-X Circle was founded by Blessing Mapalo, a visionary tech entrepreneur from Zambia with a mission to bridge the gap between African talent and global technology opportunities. What started as a personal journey of mastering code and AI has evolved into a movement.

We believe that talent is evenly distributed, but opportunity is not. Our circle was formed to change that equation - creating systems that empower builders, creators, and innovators across Africa and beyond.

Our Purpose

To create a global ecosystem where technology builders can collaborate, learn, and build wealth-generating systems together. We're not just teaching code - we're building empires.

Our Philosophy

Combining the disciplined mindset of Andrew Tate, the innovative vision of Elon Musk, and the relentless African grind. We build systems that work while others wait for opportunities.

Future Vision

To become Africa's leading tech innovation hub, launching 100+ tech startups, training 10,000+ developers, and creating a $100M+ ecosystem by 2030.

Leadership

Blessing Mapalo - Founder & Visionary

With a background in software engineering, AI development, and system architecture, Blessing leads the Circle with a unique blend of technical expertise and entrepreneurial vision. His "Build, Don't Wait" philosophy drives our community forward.
